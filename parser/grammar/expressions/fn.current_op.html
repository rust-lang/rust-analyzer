Binding powers of operators for a Pratt parser.

See https://matklad.github.io/2020/04/13/simple-but-powerful-pratt-parsing.html

Note that Rust doesn’t define associativity for some infix operators (e.g. == and ..) and requires parentheses to disambiguate. We just treat them as left associative.
